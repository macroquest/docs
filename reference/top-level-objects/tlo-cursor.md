# `Cursor`

Creates an object which references the item on your cursor.

## Forms

* [_item_](../data-types/datatype-item.md) **Cursor**

## Examples

`/if (${Cursor.ID}) /autoinv`

_If something is on your cursor, auto-inventory it._
