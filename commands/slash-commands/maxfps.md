# Maxfps

## Syntax

**/maxfps \[ bg \| fg \] \[ \# \]**

## Description

Sets the foreground or background framerate.

## Example

```text
 /maxfps fg 30            Sets foreground framerate to 30 FPS
 /maxfps bg 5             Sets background framerate to 5 FPS
```
