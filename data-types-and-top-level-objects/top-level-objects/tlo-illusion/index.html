    TLO:Illusion¶

    Used to get information about items on your illusions keyring.

    Forms¶
    __keyringitem__ Illusion[**N]** Retrieves the item in your illusions keyring by index
    __keyringitem__ Illusion[**name]** Retrieve the item in your illusions keyring by name. A = can be prepended for an exact match.
    __keyring__ Illusion Access to the illusions keyring

    Examples¶

    See: keyring and keyringitem__
